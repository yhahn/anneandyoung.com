Things to do in Syracuse, NY

Do

  • Explore Armory Square
  • Shop at Carousel Mall

  • Relax at Thornden Park and Rose Garden

  • Tea at Roji

Eat

By car

Directions from the west (I90E), east (I90W), and south (81N) on Google Maps.

By air

Fly in to Syracuse Hancock Airport. A free shuttle runs between the airport and the Crowne Plaza — you can call the shuttle from the hotel desk near Baggage Claim.

By bus/train

Buses and trains stop at the Syracuse Regional Transportation Center. It is a short car ride away from the Crowne Plaza.
